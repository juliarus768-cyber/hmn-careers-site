About Julia Cher and Hire Me Now

Founder-led career strategy support with a team-based approach.

Julia Cher is a Certified Resume Strategist with over 15 years of experience in employment services, career counselling, resume writing, and job search strategy. She has supported newcomers, students, career changers, professionals, youth, and clients navigating barriers to employment.

Julia’s approach is practical and market-informed. She helps clients understand how their background is perceived, which roles make sense, and how to communicate their value clearly in the Canadian labour market.
