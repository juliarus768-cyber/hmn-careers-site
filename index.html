Why Job Seekers Get Stuck

Unclear career direction

You are applying, but your target roles may not match your skills, goals, or the Canadian market.

Resume not aligned with Canadian expectations

Your resume may describe tasks instead of showing value, relevance, and fit.

Applying to the wrong roles

You may be spending time on jobs that are too broad, too senior, too junior, or poorly matched.

No structured job search plan

A strong job search needs targeting, tracking, follow-up, and interview preparation.
